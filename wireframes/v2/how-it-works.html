How It Works

A clear path from early decisions to real-world performance.

F8 reduces surprises by bringing clarity forward, validating delivery, and proving outcomes in use.

The simple version

Decisions made too early

Too little certainty at concept creates downstream risk.

Delivery drift

Scope and progress not properly evidenced.

Performance assumptions

Handover outcomes are rarely verified in use.

Our 7-step method

1. Define

Set targets: viability, programme, capex, tender risk, in-use performance.

2. Optimise

Use parametric modelling so the right scheme wins before lock-in.

3. Align

Define MEP scope and align with early-stage cost planning.

4. Develop

Deliver detailed 3D design earlier for full coordination and cost planning.

5. Integrate

Integrate 4D BIM and detailed cost plan before construction starts.

6. Assure

BIM-led validation and 4D early warnings for progress and change.

7. Prove in use

Post-occupancy evaluation and cloud-based monitoring verify outcomes.

What we need from you

Brief and project stage, drawings and constraints, model access if available, cost/tender information, and what you need to protect right now.

What you get back

Plain-English summaries for decision-makers, technical back-up where needed, and clear next steps.
