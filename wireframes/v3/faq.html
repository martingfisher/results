FAQ

Clear answers to common project-stage questions.

From scope and timing to BIM-led validation and in-use verification, these are the core questions clients ask first.

What does F8 do?

We join building performance, MEP, digital engineering and in-use verification across lifecycle stages.

When should we involve you?

Early is best, but we can add value at any stage where uncertainty is already material.

Do you replace the design team?

No. We work alongside existing teams and clarify interfaces and responsibilities.

What is BIM-led validation?

Using coordinated model evidence to validate scope, progress and change against reality.

What is 4D modelling?

Linking model elements to programme sequencing where it adds delivery control value.

How do we start?

Share role, stage, risk focus, and available information via project submission form.
