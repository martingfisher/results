approach:
  heading: "Strong copy places the customer at the centre of the story."
  intro: "One of the biggest messaging problems businesses face is this: they become the hero of their own story.\n\nCustomers do not usually care about the internal structure of the business, the history of the company or a long list of features. They care about their problem, the outcome they want, whether the business understands them and whether the process feels trustworthy and straightforward.\n\nThat is why we often work using StoryBrand principles. The customer becomes the central character. Because clarity almost always outperforms complexity."
