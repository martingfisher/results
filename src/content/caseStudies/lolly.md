Lolly is one of the UK's most established hospitality technology businesses, combining EPoS, self-serve kiosks, kitchen management and cloud-based reporting across sectors from coffee shops and pubs to education, healthcare and large-scale events.

The project brought together RYCM, design specialists Phunk and copywriter Matthew Coffey to create a Webflow website that could handle the full breadth of the Lolly offer - without becoming a catalogue. The structure prioritises speed of understanding: clear product routes, sector-specific entry points and proof built around scale, awards and real customer numbers.

The result is a site that earns trust quickly and gives every visitor - from an independent operator to a procurement lead at a contract caterer - a clear route into what Lolly can do for them.
