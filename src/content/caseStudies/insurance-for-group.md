Insurance For Group needed to rationalise a growing set of specialist insurance brands without losing the focus that made each one valuable.

RYCM reviewed the services, audiences and customer journeys, then created a clearer structure for the group brand and satellite websites. The work combined positioning, copy, visual direction and website delivery across a complex set of products.

The result was a web presence that made the offer easier to understand from a client point of view, while giving the business room to add and expand services without overcomplicating the journey.
