BrightBird exists because most managers find managing people the hardest part of the job — and very few have been properly trained to do it.

Karen built BrightBird around a simple idea: that leaders in education, business, charities and social enterprises deserve practical support, not management theory. She is particularly known for helping managers approach difficult conversations with confidence — the kind of conversations most people defer until the situation gets worse.

RYCM shaped the brand identity, site structure and copy to match the audience BrightBird actually serves: busy managers who know they need help, can identify the problem and want a straightforward route to a solution.

The website was designed to work across every sector BrightBird operates in, without feeling generic. Each audience — schools, businesses, charities — has its own entry point and its own language. The brand identity keeps everything coherent: approachable but professional, warm but direct.

The result is a site that reflects what Karen does in practice. It moves people from recognition to action, and makes it easy to take the first step.
